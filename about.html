Who We Are

Dehix is a verified talent provider platform where we not only provide verified freelancer profiles but also verify employers for the sake of safety. We use blockchain technology to solve the problems that come with remote work culture. Take consultant help to get your web2 project converted to Web3. Additionally, we ensure that everyone has a fair chance to get their dream job.

Wealth?

Our platform uses oracles, smart contracts, and decentralized protocols to ensure secure, transparent transactions and profile verifications.
